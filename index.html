🕌 About Our Village

Ashraf Nagar is a peaceful and beautiful rural area located in the Bhagalpur district of Bihar. Known for its strong sense of community, unity, and Islamic cultural values, the village is a symbol of harmony and mutual respect. With lush greenery, traditional homes, and a serene environment, Ashraf Nagar offers a blend of simplicity and aspiration. The people here are hardworking, friendly, and deeply rooted in their faith and traditions We aim to connect everyone — from local residents to those who’ve moved away — through community events, digital platforms, and shared goals. Together, we dream of transforming Ashraf Nagar into a model village where tradition meets technology, and every citizen contributes to growth and well-being.
