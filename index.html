🕌 About Our Village

Ashraf Nagar is a peaceful and beautiful rural area located in Bhagalpur district of Bihar. Known for its unity, Islamic culture, and development potential, we aim to connect everyone and grow together.

Education

  • Madrasas and schools for literacy
  • Digital learning & tuition centers

Agriculture & Livelihood

  • Modern farming methods
  • Organic training & market links

Health & Well-being

  • Free health camps
  • Nutrition & emergency awareness

Women Empowerment

  • Self-help groups
  • Training for home business

Youth Engagement

  • Sports & career events
  • Village youth forums

Clean Village Drive

  • Plantation & weekly cleaning
  • Proper waste management
